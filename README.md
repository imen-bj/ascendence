## 📖 Story & Lore

**Erenora (Meaning: "Shining Light" / "Compassion")**  
Our protagonist, Erenora, is a modern-day enthusiast of ancient Greek mythology. Her life changes forever when she visits an authentic historical site and discovers a mysterious artifact. Upon touching it, she is unexpectedly plunged into a reimagined mythical world and transported straight to **Mount Olympus**.

There, she is summoned by the King of the Gods himself: **Zeus**.

Zeus gives her a terrifying ultimatum—complete a perilous task for him, or perish in this ancient realm. Despite her desperate pleas to return to her normal life, she is forced to accept his quest. Her mission? To battle and defeat **Prometheus**, the first major adversary of the game.

In this reshaped mythological universe, Prometheus has grown vastly stronger than Zeus. Terrified of losing his throne and knowing he cannot win in a direct fight, the cowardly Zeus intends to use Erenora to eliminate him and claim Prometheus's heart/power.

**The Twist:** Erenora's journey begins as she sets out to battle Prometheus. But once she achieves the impossible and claims Prometheus's power, she realizes she doesn't have to be a pawn anymore. Empowered, she turns her sights on the King of the Gods himself, deciding to battle Zeus and claim the throne to become the God of Gods!

### 🎭 Key Characters
* **Erenora (Main Character):** A courageous mythology enthusiast unexpectedly thrown into a world of myth. She must survive and seize her own destiny.
* **Prometheus (Level 1 Boss):** A legendary figure whose immense power threatens the throne of Olympus. You must claim his power to survive.
* **Zeus (Level 2 Boss):** The manipulative God of Thunder who forced Erenora into this world. He will become her ultimate, climactic challenge.

## 🌟 Key Features

* **🏃‍♂️ Fluid & Dynamic Movement:** Master the art of traversal with double jumps, directional dashing, and responsive physics.
* **⚔️ Engaging Combat System:** Face off against enemies with varied AI behaviors. Execute combos and utilize knockback mechanics to survive.

* **🗺️ Interactive Environments:** Navigate levels filled with moving, bouncing, and breakable platforms, alongside scattered collectibles (coins, gems, and power-ups).
* **👹 Epic Boss Battles:** Test your skills against complex, multi-phase boss encounters.

## 🛠️ Technical Overview

* **Language:** C
* **Graphics & Audio:** SDL 1.2, SDL_image, SDL_mixer, SDL_ttf
* **Engine Features:**
  * Custom physics and gravity processing
  * Multi-state sprite animation system
  * Advanced bounding-box collision detection
  * Enemy AI pathing and state machines (Idle, Walking, Attacking, Stunned)

## 🎮 How to Play

### Compilation

Ensure you have a GCC compiler and the necessary SDL libraries installed (`libsdl1.2-dev`, `libsdl-image1.2-dev`, `libsdl-mixer1.2-dev`, `libsdl-ttf2.0-dev`).

To build the game, simply run:
```bash
make
```

### Execution
Start your adventure with:
```bash
make run
```
*(Or execute `./game` directly)*

### 🕹️ Controls
* **Movement:** Right / Left Arrow Keys 
* **Jump :** Spacebar
* **Climb:** `J`+ Up Keys
* **Attack / Combos:** `K` Key 
* **Menu Interaction:** Mouse & Click
